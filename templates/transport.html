City and Intercity Transport

  • Marshrutki (minibuses) — cheap and common inside cities.
  • Bus & trolleybus — public options in major cities like Bishkek.
  • Taxi Apps — Yandex Go, NambaTaxi widely used.
  • Intercity shared taxis — Bishkek ↔ Karakol / Issyk-Kul etc.
